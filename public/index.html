GENESIS 2023

Department of Computer Application

"GENESIS" symbolizes the "Rise of the Next Generation." The world keeps advancing when it comes to Technology: Typewriters were replaced by Computers, Telephones by Mobile Phones. "GENESIS" will compromise of various "on-stage" and "off-stage" competitions in the field of IT. This is a learning process by which student will gain an additional knowledge and experience under various aspects in the IT field such as coding, gaming, Photoshop, PC assembling, etc. This will boost the morale and confidence of the students to compete in the current and future IT Field. This will help them to get extra experience in the field of their current study which would act as added study reference to the students. They will get exposure to practical, in a competitive way.

General Rules

There are points for Early Birds!

  • Fee Rs-1500/- per team. Max 20 participants per college for Technical & Cultural events.
  • Registration fee of Rs-1500 to be paid on the day of the event in college.
  • 5 Separate participants for the gaming event, Students participating in Gaming event will not be allowed to participate in Technical & Cultural events.
  • College Id is mandatory for registration. Judges decision will be final and binding. Early birds will get 50 points.
  • If the team participant is found to be indisciplined or violates the code of conduct, the Team will be disqualified.
  • Maintain decency and decorum.
  • Your cooperation is the key to success. Participants are responsible for their belongings, College will not be held responsible.
  • If team fails to participate in any event then -5 points.
  • Every event, including the Gaming event will also have points.
  • Only the first top 10 colleges will be allowed.
